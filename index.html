Principles

What we believe

Agency over automation

The goal is not to replace what you do, but to expand what you can do. Every interaction should reinforce your sense of ownership and capability.

Understand first, act second

No agent should execute without understanding your context, constraints, and intent. Research before building. Ask before assuming.

Do it for them, not to them

When the system recommends a tool, it should set it up — not hand you a guide. The agent handles the tedium; you make the decisions.

Compounding context

Every conversation deepens the system's understanding. Cross-domain patterns emerge. The mediation layer gets better the longer you use it.

Human in the loop, always

You approve every plan before execution. The system proposes, you decide. No silent delegation, no irreversible actions without consent.
